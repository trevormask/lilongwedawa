Our Impact: Celebrating Faith and Serving Humanity

From the joyful atmosphere of Eid to the reflective peace of our Dawa programs, every LEDYOC activity is designed to empower the youth of Lilongwe with knowledge and community spirit.

We believe Eid is a time for communal joy and unity. Our events bring thousands of youth and families together for prayer, feasting, and entertainment in a safe, Islamic environment.

Eid ul-Fitr 2025: Grand Iftar and Games

The committee organized a mass Iftar for 500+ youth, followed by inspirational talks and supervised recreational games at the Area 25 grounds.

**Focus:** Unity, Gratitude, and Post-Ramadan Consistency.

Eid ul-Adha 2024: Qurbani Outreach Project

In partnership with local mosques, LEDYOC facilitated the distribution of Qurbani meat to 150 needy families in the surrounding Lilongwe townships, ensuring everyone shared in the blessings.

**Focus:** Sacrifice, Charity (Sadaqah), and Community Care.

Our Dawa programs are the core of our educational efforts, fostering intellectual and spiritual growth among the youth.

The Lilongwe Youth Halaqah (Weekly)

A consistent weekly study circle focusing on Fiqh, Seerah, and Quranic interpretation. Held every Saturday evening at the committee office.

**Current Topic:** The Ethics of Social Media in Islam.

Street Dawa Initiative (Monthly)

Monthly outreach where youth volunteers distribute Islamic literature and free refreshments, answering questions from the public in the Lilongwe Old Town Market area.

**Impact:** Reached over 1,000 community members this quarter.
