Our Story: Serving the Youth of Lilongwe

The Lilongwe Eid & Dawa Youth Organisation Committee (LEDYOC) was established in 2018 by a group of passionate young Malawian Muslims who saw a need for more organized, spiritually-grounded, and socially impactful youth activities. We began by simply organizing communal Eid prayers and meals and have since grown into a pillar of the community, focusing equally on spiritual growth (Dawa) and civic engagement.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading platform for Muslim youth empowerment, unity, and holistic development in the capital city of Lilongwe and beyond.

Mission

To implement high-quality educational programs, facilitate annual, memorable Eid celebrations, and conduct consistent humanitarian projects in line with Islamic values.
