Our Core Mission

1. Eid Festivities: To organize uplifting and unifying Eid ul-Fitr and Eid ul-Adha celebrations that foster brotherhood and sisterhood among the youth.

2. Dawa & Education: To facilitate educational workshops, study circles, and public outreach programs to promote a better understanding of Islam.

3. Community Service: To inspire social responsibility through targeted charity drives and community cleanup initiatives in Lilongwe.
